How to reheat Food
in the Microwave...

Scroll down below to view a 10 step guide on how to reheat food in the microwave.
To begin press any button on the keypad
Scroll down to view the steps written out!
1
Make sure the microwave is on
2
Open the microwave
3
Place your food in the microwave
4
Close the Microwave door
5
Type in the desired heating time
6
Wait until time is up
7
Open the Microwave once the food is done heating
8
If the food is not warm enough, place it back in the Microwave
9
Once the food is warm enough, take it out of the microwave
10
And enjoy!
