A STEP BY STEP GUIDE
ON HOW TO USE A
MICROWAVE
Need a quick and simple way to heat up your food? Microwaves are a great option! They're quick, convienient, and very easy to use!
STEP 1:
OPEN THE MICROWAVE
STEP 2:
PLACE YOUR FOOD IN THE MICROWAVE
STEP 3:
CLOSE THE DOOR
STEP 4:
TYPE IN THE DESIRED HEATING TIME
STEP 5:
PRESS START
STEP 6:
WAIT...
STEP 7:
ONCE TIME'S UP...
STEP 8:
OPEN THE MICROWAVE
STEP 9:
TAKE OUT YOUR FOOD
(CAREFUL IT'S HOT!)
STEP 10:
AND ENJOY!
